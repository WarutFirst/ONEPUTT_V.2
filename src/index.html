Features

Practice with different green speed

With the option to choose green speed, improve your weight control and make you ready for next real course.

Multiple Practice Mode

Make focus on each practice session with straightness, or weight control.

Launch angle detect

The only Infared putting training system that can measure launch angle, making putting distance calculation more accurate.

Data makes improvement

On practice mode, you see the result from each shot helping to choose what to focus on your next session.

Practice anywhere

Portable and light weight putting matt, required minimal space for practice.

Virtual putt green

Play on an 18-holes putt green to further practice green reading skill with fun.

Improve your putt

Wireless Practice
